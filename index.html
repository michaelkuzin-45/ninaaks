Meet Dr. Nina Aks, DMD

A commitment to excellence, honesty, and compassionate care.

Portrait of Dr. Aks

Welcome to Aks Dental. My philosophy is simple: treat every patient the way I would want my own family to be treated.

With over two decades of experience in dentistry, I have dedicated my career to mastering the latest techniques to ensure my patients receive the highest standard of care. I earned my Doctor of Medicine in Dentistry (DMD) degree and continually pursue advanced education in comprehensive and cosmetic procedures.

What truly drives our practice is the relationships we build. Many of our patients have been with us for years, bringing in their children and eventually their grandchildren. We view ourselves as partners in your health journey, ensuring every visit is bright, pleasant, and entirely fear-free.

Our Practice Philosophy

  • Honesty First We only recommend treatments that are truly necessary for your oral health and well-being.
  • Gentle Touch We go out of our way to make sure every procedure is as comfortable and anxiety-free as possible.
  • Unrushed Care We schedule ample time for appointments so you never feel hurried, and we always answer your questions.
